Environment supported by various
Financial Aid Programs (acquired in 2020 - 2023)

With approximately 154 billion KRW each year for government-funded education/research projects, private research projects, etc., CBNU has focused on fostering creative, interdisciplinary talent. In addition, CBNU is dedicated to establishing a sustainable research ecosystem to be a leading university in Korea with global competitiveness.
